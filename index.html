Teeth Whitening

Dental veneers are a great way to permanently whiten your teeth, so no more white strips and no need for whitening treatments.

Dental Cleaning

A dental cleaning is typically a painless procedure because there is no cutting or injecting involved.

Teeth Extraction

Tooth extraction offers a number of benefits. Most importantly, it reduces harmful bacteria that can damage your teeth and gums.

Dental Implants

The average lifespan of a dental implant is anywhere from 10 -30 years. This means that most likely, depending on your age when you get an implant, it will last for the rest of your life.

Root Canal Treatment

Teeth that receive a root canal, and a subsequent filling and crown last about 20 years. Teeth that receive either a filling or a crown after a root canal last around 11 years.

Orthodontics

Orthodontics is the branch of dentistry that focuses on diagnosing and treating “bad bites” (malocclusion). Common orthodontic treatments include braces, clear aligners, and retainers.
